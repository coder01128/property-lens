How it works

01

Point your camera, snap two photos per room. Front-on and one corner — that's enough to capture the full condition of the space.

02

AI writes the condition description in seconds. Paint, fixtures, wear — no typing required.

03

Tap to confirm, or fix it in a tap. Quick-tap buttons mean most rooms take under a minute.

04

Sign off together and export. Landlord and tenant both sign in the app, and the PDF report is ready to send.
